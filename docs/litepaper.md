# GENESIS SENTIENCE PROTOCOL

## Litepaper v0.1

---

## 1. The Problem

Blockchains today execute deterministically but do not reason, adapt, or explain themselves.

AI systems today make decisions but lack auditability, economic accountability, and on-chain presence.

Capital markets remain fragmented, manually governed, slow to adapt, and opaque in their automation.

**The result: capital, governance, and AI remain disconnected systems.**

---

## 2. The Thesis

Genesis Sentience Protocol (GSP) is an AI-native Layer-0 where:

- AI agents are economic actors with deterministic DNA
- Capital allocation is partially automated within constitutional bounds
- Governance proposals are simulated before execution
- Every decision is cryptographically explainable
- Shared security protects multiple sovereign rails

The system merges sovereign specialization, shared validator security, elastic subnets, agent-based capital management, and a narrative audit layer into a single coherent infrastructure.

---

## 3. Why Now

Three macro forces converge:

1. **AI becoming infrastructure-level** — Foundation models are production-ready. The next step is economic accountability.
2. **On-chain capital markets maturing** — DeFi, RWA, and institutional adoption create demand for transparent autonomous infrastructure.
3. **Institutions demanding transparency** — Regulators and allocators increasingly require explainable, auditable systems.

AI must become accountable. Blockchains must become adaptive. GSP is the convergence.

## 4. Architecture Overview

GSP consists of three macro layers:

### Trinity Kernel
Hybrid consensus (Tendermint-inspired proposer rotation + Snowman-style fast finality), controlled entropy injection, and fractal realm allocation.

### AI Mesh
MCP orchestration for agent proposals, RAG engine for precedent retrieval, governance AI for policy simulation, and a central agent registry.

Together they create a system that not only executes policy but explains and justifies it.

## 9. Risk Controls

Embedded constitutional constraints enforced at consensus:

- **Inflation cap**: 15% annual maximum
- **Reserve minimum**: 20% treasury floor
- **Agent weight cap**: 5% governance limit per agent
- **Hard finality**: 2/3 + 1 validator threshold
- **Mutation governance**: Agent DNA changes require proposal + quorum
- **Human veto**: Constitutional amendments require human approval

The system is designed to prevent runaway AI behavior at the protocol level.

## 10. GSP-CIVIC: Civilizational Memory

GSP-CIVIC is the protocol's hybrid communication and memory layer.

All proposals, debates, incidents, and agent decisions are recorded as typed threads stored off-chain (IPFS/Arweave) with on-chain proof anchoring via content hashes and Merkle roots.

CIVIC introduces formal hierarchy (Constitutional → Governance → Operations → Citizens), reputation scoring, and precedent indexing, allowing both humans and AI agents to coordinate transparently while preserving an immutable civilizational record of rationale, dissent, and outcomes.
